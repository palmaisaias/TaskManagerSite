Bachelor Party Weekend Finalize the itinerary and make any last-minute reservations for the bachelor party weekend to ensure everything goes smoothly. Pack all necessary items, including clothes, gifts, and any special equipment needed for planned activities. Coordinate with friends to arrange transportation and set meeting points for the trip to the bachelor party destination.

Visit Parents Call or message your parents to confirm the visit and discuss any plans or activities they have in mind. Prepare a small gift or thoughtful gesture to bring along, showing appreciation for their hospitality. Plan your travel route and schedule, ensuring you arrive on time and make the most of your visit with your parents.

Complete Application Project Review the project requirements and create a checklist of tasks to ensure all aspects of the application are covered. Dedicate focused time blocks each day to work on the project, aiming to complete one major task or milestone daily. Test the application thoroughly, fix any bugs, and prepare the final presentation or documentation for submission.

Prep and Travel to Yosemite Research and plan the hiking trails and activities you want to do in Yosemite, making note of any permits or reservations needed. Pack essential gear and supplies for the trip, including camping equipment, hiking gear, and sufficient food and water. Check the weather forecast and road conditions, and map out your travel route to ensure a safe and timely arrival at Yosemite.
